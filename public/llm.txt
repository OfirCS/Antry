# Antry

Antry is a proof-of-work network for AI builders.

## What Antry does

- Companies post real Briefs.
- Builders solve those Briefs in an instrumented Lab.
- Antry mints signed Receipts that capture prompts, tool calls, pivots, outputs, cost, duration, and behavioral signal.
- Receipts help companies evaluate how builders think, not only what they say on a resume.

## Useful routes

- Builders: /builders
